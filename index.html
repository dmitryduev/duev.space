Research

My research interests cover a wide range of topics across astroinformatics, optical, and infrared astronomy:

  • Distributed data processing systems, large databases, high performance computing, GPU computing, machine/deep learning, mathematical methods in data processing and analysis
  • Interferometric and Doppler measurements of spacecraft. Standard, near-field and space VLBI, signal delay modelling, propagation effects, phase calibration methods, imaging. VLBI and Doppler data processing and analysis for the use in planetary science, interplanetary plasma physics, geodesy, and fundamental physics
  • Astrometry of asteroids with adaptive optics, imaging of Solar system planets with adaptive optics
